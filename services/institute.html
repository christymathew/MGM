PARAMEDICAL INSTITUTE


MGM Paramedical Institute is the leading Proffessional Educational Institution in Kottayam District Since 1963. Thousands of students studied and trained here and all are working all over India and abroad. We conduct Diploma courses under the application of Bharat Sevak Samaj (BSS) National Development Agency promoted by Government of India.

Eligibility Criteria

Secondary School examination (10th standard) with minimum 45% marks from either the Kerala State Education Board, the Central Board for Secondary Education (CBSE), or the Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE), or equivalent examination from a recognized Board/University. All SC/ST candidates have a relaxation of 5% in the minimum aggregate.
