About

MGM Clinical laboratory is the foremost Health Care Solutions provider in Kottayam town. The preventive health care of MGM is easily accessible, accurate and affordable. The semi-automated and computerized MGM Laboratory bestows necessary patient care facilities in a customer friendly manner.

We provide qualitative and quantitative analysis of biological and aspirated fluids such as blood, serum or plasma, tissue, urine, stool, C.S.F. etc. We also ensure diagnostic medical imaging services such as digital X-Ray with enhanced images.

We replenish outstanding healthcare services along with remarkable technical support. More than 200+ tests are done and accurate results are provided by our team. The skilled and disciplined staff crew of MGM takes the responsibilities of the bio-medical equipment handling and maintenance. Skill oriented training programs are provided for our staff for the performance enhancement.
