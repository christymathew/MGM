About

MGM Paramedical Institute is the leading Professional Educational Institution in Kottayam District with the experience more than five decades. We deliver high quality and integrated medical education for bringing out skilled and disciplined paramedics. Thousands of students studied and trained here and all are working all over India and abroad.

We conduct Diploma courses under the application of Bharat Sevak Samaj (BSS) National Development Agency promoted by Government of India. Excellent infrastructure and faculties of the institution helps the aspirant to acquire knowledge in latest technologies and development in the field with professionalism. The practical training programs for the students are provided with reputed institutions and cent percentage placement assurance is offered.

Eligibility Criteria

Secondary School examination (10th standard) with minimum 45% marks from either the Kerala State Education Board, the Central Board for Secondary Education (CBSE), or the Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE), or equivalent examination from a recognized Board/University. All SC/ST candidates have a relaxation of 5% in the minimum aggregate.
