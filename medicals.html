About

MGM Medicals was established with a few loyal customers at the heart of Kottayam Town on 2002. It started as the fulfillment of a dream nurtured deep in the heart to the express purpose of making it safer, convenient & affordable for consumers to purchase medicines and continues its legacy of 17 years of success in the pharmaceutical industry. The systematically organized structure and focus of the team on success lead us through the journey over the years with the core belief for enabling patients to restart their healthier lives.
